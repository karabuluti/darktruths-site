DarkTruths Automation

DarkTruths Automation is a private creator workflow for preparing short-form quote videos and uploading approved TikTok drafts for manual editing and posting.

How it works

The creator connects TikTok through Login Kit. Approved short-form videos are uploaded to TikTok as drafts/inbox uploads using the Content Posting API. The creator then opens TikTok, reviews the draft, optionally selects native TikTok music, edits if needed, and manually publishes the post.
