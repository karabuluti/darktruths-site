Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Information collected

DarkTruths Automation may process basic TikTok account information provided through Login Kit, such as open ID, display name, and authorization tokens needed to upload approved drafts.

2. How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate the creator account and upload approved short-form videos to TikTok as drafts/inbox uploads through the Content Posting API.

3. Publishing

The app does not automatically publish TikTok videos in the draft workflow. The creator manually reviews, edits, selects native music if desired, and publishes inside TikTok.

4. Data sharing

Data is not sold. Data is shared with TikTok only as required for authentication and draft upload.

5. Data storage

Authorization credentials are stored privately by the creator's automation environment and are not exposed publicly.

6. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the project owner through the GitHub account associated with this site.
